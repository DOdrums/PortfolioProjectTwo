What is it?

Welcome to the Moonlander Typing Tutor. Have you acquired a fresh new Moonlander keyboard by ZSA, but find it hard to actually type on the darn thing? This is the place to be! Below you'll find a typing tutor that you can use to practice your typing skills with either text or code!

How does it work?

Select 'Text' or 'Code' and you are ready to type! The little animated keyboard shows which letters you are hitting. The text will light up green when correct and red when wrong. Backspace works, but wont reset the error counter. Your accuracy is calculated with the error count and you can also see your words per minute! Hit the little reset button to start over.
